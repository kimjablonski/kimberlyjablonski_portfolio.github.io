Electric Forest

poster series

The objective of this project was to create a poster series that captured the atmosphere that the music festival ‘Electric Forrest’ created. Through these posters I tried to recreate and represent the unique led light show that the festival is known for. The lights are flashed and hung in trees, lights show dance while bands play; making the forest feel vibrant, alive and electric. The festival is broken into two weekends, so I dedicated each side poster to the band that will play that weekend.
