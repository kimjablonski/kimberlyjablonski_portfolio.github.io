Peggy’s Porkers

t-shirt design

This t-shirt design was done for a club who wanted some fun and standout team shirts. They came up with the team ‘Peggy’s Porkers’ based off of a stuffed animal pig that one of the members had. The goal was to create a unique team shirt that revolved around the pig. This design allowed me to be illustrative with the design, and playful by adding the tail to the back of the shirt.
